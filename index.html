Meet the Founder  

Music & Film From Harlem streets to global streams—music, film, and podcasts that move the culture

TSW brings a dynamic edge to music and film production, driven by founder Tariq Alexander’s extensive industry experience. With in-house podcasting capabilities, the company creates original content that blends entertainment, culture, and innovation. From film projects to music production, TSW is committed to delivering high-quality creative work that resonates with today’s audiences and reflects the energy of its Harlem roots.

Retail & Lifestyle High-end looks, knockout workouts, and plant-powered living—all at your fingertips

TSW’s retail and lifestyle offerings combine luxury, wellness, and community under one roof. Shoppers can access exclusive, high-end items at unbeatable liquidation prices, making premium living affordable. The lifestyle experience extends beyond retail with O’s Boxing Gym, featuring world-class trainers, and The Herbacy, providing holistic nutrition and wellness options. Together, these elements create a space where fitness, fashion, and nourishment meet to empower everyday living.

Meet the Founder

What We Do  

Founder: Tariq Alexander

Tariq Alexander
Where Entertainment Meets E-Commerce

Tariq Alexander is a modern day renaissance man. Over the last 20 years, Tariq Alexander has been immersed in the business of entertainment.

He began his career as a fashion model, becoming one of the first African-American males to reach an A-List status in the industry, with placements in GQ, Ebony, Vogue and with brands such as Calvin Klein, Versace, Neiman Marcus, Saks and more.

He then systematically transitioned to other areas of entertainment and media as an independent film maker, actor, producer, singer and songwriter. Alexander’s passion to help people break into the entertainment industry drove him to create a world for entertainers to connect and collaborate.

Thus, creating The System Within (TSW) to serve as a networking organization to support members by leveraging and capitalizing on emerging opportunities in the entertainment industry.
